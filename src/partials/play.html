How to Play

  • Match 3 or More Fruits

    Swap them to create juicy combos and clear obstacles.

  • Trigger Special Power-Ups

    Use boosters like the Fruit Bomb and Rainbow Splash!

  • Complete Level Challenges

    Hit score targets, collect ingredients, and clear jelly blocks.

  • Unlock New Stages

    Progress through fruit-themed worlds with increasing difficulty.

  • Compete & Win

    Climb the leaderboard and show off your puzzle skills!
