Game Overview

Welcome to Juicy Crush, the most refreshing match-3 puzzle game!

Dive into a world filled with vibrant fruits, exciting combos, and explosive juice splashes. Solve puzzles, collect rewards, and master juicy challenges in this addictively fun adventure!

Features

  • Fruit-Blasting Gameplay

    Match fruits and watch them explode in a splash of color!

  • Powerful Boosters

    Unlock special fruit combos and crushing hammers to clear tricky levels.

  • Engaging Story Mode

    Progress through hundreds of levels with exciting challenges.

  • Daily & Weekly Events

    Compete in limited-time puzzles and earn sweet rewards.

  • Offline Play

    Enjoy Juicy Crush anywhere, anytime—no internet required!

How to Play

  • Match 3 or More Fruits

    Swap them to create juicy combos and clear obstacles.

  • Trigger Special Power-Ups

    Use boosters like the Fruit Bomb and Rainbow Splash!

  • Complete Level Challenges

    Hit score targets, collect ingredients, and clear jelly blocks.

  • Unlock New Stages

    Progress through fruit-themed worlds with increasing difficulty.

  • Compete & Win

    Climb the leaderboard and show off your puzzle skills!
